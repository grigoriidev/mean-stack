Mission

Lighting of Tomorrow is an innovative, energy-efficient product & service provider that facilitates the switch from outdated, inefficient lighting to state-of-the-art LED technology. We offer lighting design consultation as well as full electrical services including inspection, installation and maintenance

L.o.T.’s Company Values:

• Superior service for every customer

• Innovation: Smart Lighting and Renewable Energy

• Positive change starts with ourselves!

Vision

Lighting of Tomorrow’s vision is to provide efficient, renewable energy solutions that serve our customers and help our planet. We believe that we have a part to play in creating a more sustainable, healthier future for our planet.

Strategic Goals

Lighting of Tomorrow’s main goal is to achieve significant energy-use reductions through the installation of energy-efficient smart LED lighting systems. We aim to illuminate our world one light fixture at a time. Our efficient and affordable smart lighting solutions allow our users to benefit from energy reduction and cost. All products and fixtures used by L.o.T. are accredited, certified, and guaranteed for commercial operation.


We believe that saving energy is simply the right thing to do. For every customer we help, we can reduce overall CO2 emissions - directly impacting our environment
